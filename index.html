How We Work

Step-1

Initial Contact With Vis Group

Our testing process starts with the initiation of your request via email or contact form on our website. At this moment we expect from you an overview of your business cases highlighting the industry and target end-users. Our team of domain experts carries out a detailed review of your request and gathers relevant requirements, technical aspects, and the ultimate goal of the testing process. After that; we provide our initial assessment of how we can contribute to your solutions from a testing and quality assurance perspective.

Step-2

Requirements Analysis and Test Planning

The second phase which starts right after the initial contact is the preparation of detailed documents related to testing plans, requirements analysis, and testing scenarios. Based on your initial request our team of talented test managers and test engineers formulates a detailed document highlighting the main requirements that need to be fulfilled at the end of the testing process. Typical activities in this step include requirement specification, conformity, consistency, completeness, and redesigning of the requirements if needed. Our team of experts devotes their time to developing a test strategy and a test plan to stipulate the entire testing process, from defining testing objectives, a testing schedule, and a test team lineup for executing test cases.

Step-3

Test Execution and Monitoring

The third step where actual action takes place is the execution phase,where of all selected test cases in different scenarios. This phase starts with the development and refinement of the test cases. Right after the test cases generation test cases execution starts. The test cases are executed and updated while keeping in mind the requirements specification and testing scenarios.

Step-4

Results Reporting

The last phase in our testing process is the result interpretation and documentation process. Our test engineers closely monitor the outcome of the execution of test cases and record all the data and logs. Upon completion of the test execution process, all the data and test outcomes are transformed into a comprehensive test results document, depicting the outcome of the tests and possible recommendations & solutions.
